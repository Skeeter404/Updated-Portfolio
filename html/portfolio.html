Link to Application

Yoso Shoppingclose

Yoso is an acronym for "You Only Shop Once". It is a smart shopping application to aid in making grocery shopping a smoother experience.

The idea was to create something to make grocery shopping easier, by automatically updating your shopping list with items that you may need, based on previous usage of an item. We also added an API where you can search for recipes and add the ingredients needed directly to your list.
