About Me

My name is Bronson Bird. I have always had a love for computers, the web, and what makes them work. Because of this, I found myself exploring different career fields that seemed more in line with the things that fascinated me. That's when I became intrigued by the world of coding and web development. At that point, I decided to dive in and enroll myself into a Coding Bootcamp through the University of Utah. The idea of creating something amazing, for potentially millions of people, was incredibly exciting for me.

Experience

Enrolling in the Coding Bootcamp gave me a ton of experience in Full Stack Development. I learned everything needed to create amazing web pages using HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery, Node, Express, Handlebars, SQL, MongoDB, and React; As well as introduced me to fantastic libraries on the web, such as Bootstrap, and Materialize.
